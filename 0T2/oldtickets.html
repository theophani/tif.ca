There are two Alumni events this year:

Each $30 ticket to the Wednesday March 13 Alumni Reunion includes admission to the catered reception and a reserved seat for the performance. The reception starts at 6pm and the show begins at 8pm. All alumni are welcome.

The Saturday March 16 Matinee Skule(tm) Nite Alumni Reunion is specifically aimed at those alumni who participated in the show during their skule days. Some answers to frequently wondered questions:

How do I buy tickets?
You can buy Skule(tm) Nite tickets using Visa or Mastercard using our online ticket-purchasing system. You can also purchase tickets from the Hart House Theatre Box Office (416-978-8668), located at 7 Hart House Circle. You may wish to view the seating plan before calling or visiting the HH Box Office.

When do tickets go on sale?
Tickets are on sale! Buy them now.

Can my club buy a block of tickets?
Groups such as University of Toronto engineering division clubs, alumni and residence clubs will be offered blocked reservations so that members of the groups can enjoy the show together. Attending the show as a group is a great theme for a social event. Groups will be contacted by our Skule(tm) and Campus Promotions before general ticket sales. We also welcome any inquiries through email.

I heard that f!rosh get free tickets. Give me my ticket.
That's not a question, but thank you for your enthusiastic interest. Engineering F!rosh have already paid for their tickets if they purchased a f!rosh kit. F!rosh can order their tickets using the regular electronic system by supplying their student numbers. Order early since availability is not guaranteed.

Where is the theatre?
Skule(tm) Nite performs at Hart House Theatre located on the University of Toronto St. George Campus. The address is 7 Hart House Circle, and the box office phone number is 416-978-8668.

We're a "Get Staged" event! Save 20%!

Do you love going to the theatre? Do you wish you knew more about arts performances that are happening on campus? Why don't you "Get Staged?"

Our venue, Hart House Theatre, will be offering memberships under a new program called "Get Staged". Card holders get a discount of 20% the ticket price of participating "Get Staged" events. Members also receive updates about the plays, concerts and lectures that will grace the stage of the historic Hart House Theatre located on the University of Toronto campus.

Proceeds from the sale of memberships go towards promoting and preserving our beloved theatre. Student "Get Staged" memberships are only $10 and can be purchased at the Hart House Theatre Box Office, located at 7 Hart House Circle. Call for more details: 416-979-8668.

Skule(tm) Nite 2002 is a "Get Staged" event. Members are eligible for a 20% discount on the price of ticket for both the Wednesday and Saturday Matinee shows*. Get Staged! discounted tickets can only be purchased at the Hart House Theatre Box Office.




* The "Get Staged" members' discount applies only to the Wed March 13 8:00pm and Sat March 16 2:00pm shows. Thursday, Friday and Saturday evening shows are ineligible for the discounted price.

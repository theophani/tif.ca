Skule(tm) Nite costs more money to stage than we make in ticket sales. We've always been able to afford our cheap ticket price through generous donations from sponsors. Every sponsor gets our thanks in the form of an advertisement in our programme, and especially generous sponsors have their logo placed on our posters and website. Sponsors are also offered complimentary tickets and are invited to the reception that follows the opening night performance.

If you are interested in sponsoring our show and would like more details please contact our Sponsorship Coordinator, Marion Hill, at skulenite@skule.ca.

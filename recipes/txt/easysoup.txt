Easy Vegetable And Chicken Soup 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1 can (28oz/796mL) tomatoes with herbs and spices 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1 can (12oz/345mL) kernel corn, drained 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1 cup fresh or canned chicken broth, optional 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1 to 2 tsp chili powder, optional 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1 cup diced cooked chicken or turkey 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1/2 to 1 tsp salt, optional 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Tortilla chips or grated cheese 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Simmer tomatoes and corn for 5 minutes then thin with broth if desired.
Add chili powder if using. Simmer 2 minutes. Stir in chicken and simmer 2
minutes or until heated through. Check seasonings, adding salt if needed.
Spoon into heated bowls and top with crumbled tortilla chips. Makes 6
cups.

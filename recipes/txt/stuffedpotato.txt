Twice Baked Potato

1/4 cup Sealtest Sour Cream                                          
2 each Green Onions                                                 chopped
4 whole Baking Potatoes                                              
3 tbsp Sealtest 2% Milk                                             
1/2 cup Cherry Hill Old Cheddar grated

Preparation: Bake potatoes until soft. Slice in half and hollow out potato
skins leaving 1/4" shell. Mash the potato with sour cream, milk, cheese,
and green onions. Fill the potato shells and return to a 350 F oven for 15
to 20 minutes.

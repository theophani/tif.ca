From the Holiday 2003 LCBO Food & Drink:

THE FIRESIDE COCKTAIL

In a cocktail shaker, add:

3/4 oz vodka
3/4 oz Irish cream
1/2 oz white creme de cacao

Shake until frothy and strain into a martini glass rimmed with crushed chocolate.

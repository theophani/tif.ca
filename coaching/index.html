About Me

I've been in the software engineering industry for over 20 years, leading teams and building products that customers love. I've done a bit of everything: leading, product managing, designing user experiences, coding, and analyzing processes. All of this has given me a 360-degree view that I bring into my coaching sessions.

People tell me they like how I break down tricky topics and make them feel confident. Let’s team up and figure things out in a relaxed and supportive way.

My Coaching Approach

When I coach, it’s all about self-reflection and action. I listen intently to what you’re struggling with and love finding those “aha!” moments together.

We’ll dive deep into any doubts you have, figure out what standards and values truly matter to you, and I’ll share stories from my own journey to help you see things in a new light.

The goal? To help you pinpoint what success looks like for you and craft a plan to get there.

What I Offer

I coach engineering leaders at all levels, from those just stepping into leadership to seasoned veterans who want a fresh view. With all my years of experience, I understand the unique ins and outs of our industry, and I shape my coaching around the reality you face.

I also offer group sessions for teams of engineering managers to bounce ideas off each other, grow their network, tackle new problems, and shape a shared way of leading. I'm there to steer the conversation and sprinkle in some new ideas, and to encourage the team to take the lead on their own initiatives.

How to get started

  1. Book a trial session. In that 30-minute session, you’ll experience a preview of the coaching relationship.
  2. Select a package. After your trial, if you would like to continue, let me know which coaching package meets your needs, and whether you will be paying privately or through your employer.
  3. Book your first regular session. You can either schedule all your sessions at once or schedule them as you go.

Got questions? Set up a time to chat. I’m happy to answer any questions you have, address any of your doubts, or elaborate on my approach.
